The Princess Bride



This summary is broken up into the amount of pages I read in each sitting. This summary will pass on all the important information that I learned from this book without you having to spend a dozen hours reading. This is my summary of the book: The Princess Bride.



Setting:

When:

Medieval Times.

Where:

The Land of Florin is set in-between where Sweden and Germany are today.

Who:

Buttercup:

  • Autumn hair color.
  • Wintry Cream skin color.

Westley:

Prince Humperdinck:

  • Shaped like a barrel - great barrel chest with mighty barrel thighs.
  • Tall.
  • Weighed close to 250lbs.
  • Walks like a crab side-to-side.
  • Fearsome and has taken on many battles.


Pages Start-51:

  • Buttercup was born at same time that a maid named Annette was the most beautiful woman in the world. When Buttercup was 10, a woman named Aluthra in Bengal was the most beautiful woman in the world. Now Buttercup is 15. Adela Turrell is the most beautiful woman in the world.
  • "Farm Boy" does everything for her and her horse named Horse. Whenever Buttercup asks him to do something, he always says, "As you wish".
  • Buttercup's father is not very successful.

Pages 52-69:

  • Buttercup is now 17.
  • The Farm Boy's name is Westley.
  • Westley is a mutt, but Buttercup is obsessed with him.
  • Westley is leaving to go to America.
  • Westley and Buttercup made a promise to always love eachother.
  • Westley got attacked by the Dread Pirate Roberts. He "died".

Pages 73-115:

  • When Prince Humperdinck heard that the King was dying, he first thought about having to get married. Negatively.
  • Queen Bella and King Latharon are Prince Humperdinck's parents.
  • Bella is Prince Humperdinck's "evil" stepmother. She is shaped like a gumdrop and has the skin color of a raspberry. She is beloved in the kingdom.
  • Prince Humperdinck and Buttercup are getting married. Buttercup will become Princess Buttercup of Hammersmith.
  • Buttercup got kidnapped by three strange men. They said they are going to kill her.

150-186:

  • A man in black is hunting the three men. The man in black "killed" the trio. Each one separately. Including Fezzik; a giant, Inigo; a swordsman, and the Sicilian.
  • Westley is the man in black!

Pages 187-212:

  • Prince Humperdinck and his troops are looking for Buttercup.
  • Buttercup followed Westley in a ravine and now they are both stuck. They escaped by going in the Fire Swamp.
  • Westley is the Dread Pirate Roberts! - The Dread Pirate Roberts isn't one person. They change about every dozen years, but no one notices.

Pages 213-214:

  • The R.O.U.S (Rodent of Unusual Size) of the Fire Swamp usually weigh 80lbs with the speed of wolves. They are also carnivores and capable of frenzy.
  • The R.O.U.S found Westley and Buttercup and they fought Westley. Westley slashed one of them and then they all ran away.

Pages 215-233:

  • Prince Humperdinck and his troops found Buttercup and Westley, and the Prince "promised" he would bring Westley to the Revenge (the Dread Pirate Roberts' ship) and not hurt him.
  • Buttercup accepted going back to the kingdom with Prince Humperdinck.

Pages 234-247:

  • Buttercup confessed to Prince Humperdinck that she will never stop loving Westley.
  • Prince Humperdinck is the one who hired the trio.
  • The Prince and Count Rugen are secretly torturing Westley underground.

Pages 248-255:

  • Prince Humperdinck is helping Buttercup write a letter to Westley.

Pages 256-276:

  • Count Rugen made a very amazing torture device. To take away the pain, Westley thought about Buttercup.
  • Inigo and Fezzik (both from the trio) are alive!
  • Fezzik has been taking care of Inigo.
  • Inigo and Fezzik think that Westley is the Dread Pirate Roberts.
  • Inigo is planning on finding Count Rugen and avenge his father (Count Rugen killed Inigo's father).
  • Inigo and Fezzik want Westley to help them make a plan to lead them into the castle.

Pages 277-303:

  • The torture device took away 20 years of Westley's life.
  • The underground place Westley has been in is called the Zoo of Death. It has many exotic animals in it.

Pages 304-332:

  • Inigo and Fezzik found the man black "dead".
  • Inigo and Fezzik brought Westley to Miracle Max. Miracle Max wants Westley to come back because Westley can start the downfall of Prince Humperdinck.
  • Miracle Max made a pill that brought Westley back to life for 40 minutes.

Pages 333-343:

  • Buttercup was going to kill herself with a Florinese dagger but was surprised from Westley appearing and saying, "There are always too few perfect breasts in this world; leave yours alone."

Pages 344-End:

  • Inigo didn't know that Count Rugen had a Florinese dagger when he confronted him. He tried to say, "My name is Inigo Montaya; you killed my father; prepare to die." He only got out a couple words before the dagger "rearranged his insides". Inigo killed Count Rugen by trying to take out his heart.
  • Westley defeated Prince Humperdinck and tied him up.
  • Westley, Buttercup, and Inigo then left.
