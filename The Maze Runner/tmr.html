The Maze Runner

This summary is broken up into 2 chapters per section. This summary will reflect on all the important information that I learned from this book without you having to spend a dozen hours reading. This is my summary of the book: The Maze Runner.

Chapters 1 & 2 (Pages 1-12):

  • Thomas is the main character.
  • Thomas lost his memory.
  • Alby greeted Thomas. Alby was a dark-skinned boy with short-cropped hair and a face clean shaved.
  • All the boys around Thomas were mid-teens to young adults. There were about 50-60 of them.
  • Newt was taller than Alby and had long blond hair with veins sticking out of his muscled arms.
  • Chuck was a "wee little fat shank" who is in charge of Thomas' sleep arrangements.

Chapters 3 & 4 (Pages 13-28):

  • Gally was a tall, skinny boy with black hair who looked 15 or so. His nose was the size of a small fist.
  • They all live in the glade. In the glade, the farm area was spread across the entire northeast quarter, the animals were in the southeast corner, and the Box was in a hole in the very middle. There are four vast openings in the middle of the main walls.
  • Ben is going through "the changing."
  • Frypan is the chef in the glade.
  • The openings in the walls are actually big doors that close at night. The inside of the walls contain a big maze with massive stone block and thick ivy.
